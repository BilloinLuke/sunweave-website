About Huiyi

Global Supply, Local Craftsmanship

Jiangsu Huiyi Supply Chain Management Co., Ltd. is headquartered in Nantong, Jiangsu — China's textile capital. We operate as a vertically integrated manufacturer and exporter, combining R&D, intelligent production, and global logistics under one roof.

Our philosophy is simple: quality first, innovation driven, green manufacturing, shared globally. From premium fibers to finished towels, we deliver a one-stop OEM/ODM solution for brands, resorts, retailers and e-commerce sellers worldwide.

ODM / OEM

Flexible Customization, From Idea to Shelf

Whether you need a single custom print or a full private-label collection, our team supports every stage of product development.

  • 1

    Concept & Design

    Share your idea, Pantone colors or reference samples. Our designers turn concepts into production-ready artwork.

  • 2

    Material & Sampling

    Choose microfiber suede, waffle weave, organic cotton or GRS recycled fabric. Digital samples in 3–7 days.

  • 3

    Weaving & Printing

    Automated looms and digital sublimation/reactive printing ensure vivid colors and soft hand-feel.

  • 4

    Cutting & Quality Control

    Overlock stitching, 100% inspection for stains and size accuracy, needle detection.

  • 5

    Custom Packaging & Shipping

    Compression packing, FNSKU labeling, FBA palletizing and DDP delivery to global warehouses.
